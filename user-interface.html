Smart Stop is a transit app concept designed to help UC Davis students track bus schedules, check parking availability, and plan routes more efficiently. Inspired by firsthand experience, the app addresses the everyday uncertainty students face when commuting across campus by providing real-time updates, notifications, and clear wayfinding.

I developed high-fidelity prototypes that emphasized a calming color palette, streamlined navigation, and a color-coded parking system to make key information easy to scan at a glance. These choices aimed to reduce commuter stress and cognitive load while navigating transit and parking. This process strengthened my skills in user-centered interface design, layout iteration, and UX prototyping.

Skills: UX research, wireframing, prototyping, UI design, visual hierarchy, Figma, Adobe XD

Marites is a community platform designed to help Overseas Filipino Workers feel more connected and reduce loneliness by sharing personal stories and connecting with others nearby.

I focused on user-centered research and exploratory mapping to understand user needs, creating wireframes and prototypes that prioritize usability, visual hierarchy, and culturally relevant design elements. This project strengthened my skills in designing meaningful, inclusive digital experiences for diverse communities.

Skills: UX research, wireframing, prototyping, user-centered design, Figma
